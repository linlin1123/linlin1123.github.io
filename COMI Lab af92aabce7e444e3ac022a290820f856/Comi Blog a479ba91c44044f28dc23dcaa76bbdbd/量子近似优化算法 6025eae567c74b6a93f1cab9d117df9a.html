1. Introduction

With the rapid development of quantum computing, people pay more and more attention to using quantum computing to solve combinatorial optimization problems. .e maximum cut problem (MaxCut) [1] is a typical NP-hard combinatorial optimization problem in graph theory. A promising approach to solve combinatorial optimization problems on near-term machines is the quantum approximate optimization algorithm (QAOA) [2]. .is is a heuristic method, which can be considered as a time discretization of adiabatic quantum computing [3]. Like many quantum computing [4–6] methods, QAOA is a hybrid classicalquantum algorithm that combines quantum circuits and classical optimization of those circuits. .e goal of QAOA is a function of the quantum state, which in turn is a function parameterized by one- and two-qubit gates, and its parameters can be continuously varied. In 2014, Edward Farhi and Jeffrey Goldstone jointly published the QAOA for the MaxCut problem (MaxCut QAOA) algorithm [7]. .is algorithm is a polynomial-time approximate optimization algorithm for solving combinatorial optimization problems and has the potential to demonstrate quantum hegemony. However, this classical algorithm could require space doubly exponential in p, where p is the time of unitary transformation. Later, a classical computer is used as the outer-loop optimizer of quantum neural network to find the best parameters, which then were fed to the quantum computer with different sets of parameters. In 2018, Gavin proposed an algorithm [8] that used classical simulation with automatic differentiation [9] of parameters and stochastic gradient descent to explore the MaxCut QAOA problem. However, it is difficult to implement stochastic gradient descent directly on a quantum computer, since the requisite gradients are expensive to measure [10], requiring many observations for each gradient component. A variety of approaches have been used to optimize MaxCut QAOA circuits, including Nelder–Mead [11], gradient descent [12], limited-memory Broyden–Fletcher–Goldfarb–Shanno Bound (L-BFGS-B)[13], and Bayesian [14] methods. Among them, Nelder–Mead and L-BFGS-B are the local optimization methods, and gradient descent and Bayesian methods are the global optimization methods. However, these approaches are relatively poor in generalization. In other words, a classical neural network trained by these methods to optimize small quantum neural network (QNN) [15] cannot learn parameter update heuristics which can generalize to larger system sizes.

随着量子计算的飞速发展，人们越来越重视利用量子计算解决组合优化问题。MaxCut问题[1]是图论中典型的NP-hard组合优化问题。量子近似优化算法 (QAOA) [2]是解决近期机器上的组合优化问题的一种前沿方法。QAOA是一种启发式方法，可视为一种绝热量子计算的时间离散化算法[3]。QAOA与许多量子计算 [4-6] 方法类似，是一种混合经典量子算法，它结合了量子电路和这些电路的经典优化。QAOA 的目标是量子态的函数，而量子态又是一个由一个和两个量子比特门参数化的函数，其参数可以连续变化。2014 年，Edward Farhi 和 Jeffery Goldstone 联合发表了针对 MaxCut 问题的 QAOA（MaxCut QAOA）算法 [7]。该算法是一种求解组合优化问题的多项式时间近似优化算法，具有证明量子霸权的潜力。然而，这种经典算法要求在 ppp﻿ 空间是双指数的，其中 ppp﻿ 是酉变换的时间。之后，经典计算机被用作量子神经网络的外环优化器，以找到最佳参数，并将这些参数提供给具有不同参数集的量子计算机。2018 年，Gavin 提出了一种算法 [8]，该算法使用具有参数自动微分 [9] 和随机梯度下降的经典模拟来探索 MaxCut QAOA 问题。然而，直接在量子计算机上实现随机梯度下降是很困难的，因为所需的梯度测量成本很高 [10]，需要对每个梯度分量进行多次观测。用于优化 MaxCut QAOA 电路的方法有很多，包括 Nelder-Mead [11]、梯度下降法 [12]、有限记忆 Broyden-Fletcher-Goldfarb-Shanno Bound (L-BFGS-B) [13] 和贝叶斯方法[14]。其中，Nelder-Mead 和 L-BFGS-B 是局部优化方法，梯度下降法和贝叶斯方法是全局优化方法。然而，这些方法的泛化能力相对较差。换句话说，通过这些方法训练以优化小型量子神经网络（QNN）[15]的经典神经网络无法学习可以推广到更大系统规模的参数更新启发式方法。

In order to solve these problems, we propose a new local optimization method, which is the quantum approximate optimization algorithm with metalearning for the MaxCut problem (MetaQAOA). This algorithm makes use of the quantum-classical metalearning approach composed of a group of metaoptimization techniques to learn how to modify the parameters of learning algorithms to further customize them for the MaxCut QAOA problems. This could be to ensure that the learning can be well generalized, to better ﬁt the given data with fewer iterations, and to adapt a pretrained neural network model to a new task. Through the simulation experiment on TensorFlow Quantum (TFQ)[16], the convergence of the model shows that the total number of optimization iterations required to achieve a given accuracy has been signiﬁcantly improved. The experiment simulation via TensorFlow Quantum (TFQ) shows that MetaQAOA requires fewer iterations to reach the threshold of the loss function, and the threshold of the loss value after training is smaller than the other local optimization methods, i.e., Nelder–Mead and L-BFGS-B. In addition, the path chosen by the long short-term memory (LSTM) optimizer on a larger MaxCut problem after being trained on many random smaller MaxCut problems demonstrates that even when there is less data, the neural network learns to generalize its parameter update heuristic to larger system sizes. This provides the possibility of training on small, classical, and simulatable MaxCut QAOA problem instances, in order to initialize larger, classically diﬃcult to simulate MaxCut QAOA problem instances on quantum devices.

为了解决这些问题，本文提出了一种新的局部优化方法，即求解MaxCut问题的元学习量子近似优化算法（MetaQAOA）。该算法利用由一组元优化技术组成的量子经典元学习方法来学习如何修改学习算法的参数，以进一步改进 MaxCut QAOA 问题。这是为了确保学习可以很好地泛化，以更少的迭代次数来更好地拟合给定数据，并使预训练的神经网络模型适应新任务。通过在 TensorFlow Quantum (TFQ) [16] 上的仿真实验，模型的收敛性表明达到给定精度所需的优化迭代总数得到了显著提高。通过 TensorFlow Quantum (TFQ) 进行的实验模拟表明，MetaQAOA 需要较少的迭代次数即可达到损失函数的阈值，并且训练后的损失值阈值小于 Nelder-Mead 和 L- BFGS-B局部优化方法。另外，长短时记忆(LSTM)优化器在多个随机较小Maxcut问题上训练后，在较大的 MaxCut 问题上选择的路径表明，即使数据较少，神经网络也会学习泛化其参数更新启发式推广到更大的系统规模。这提供了在小型、经典和可模拟的 MaxCut QAOA 问题实例上进行训练的可能性，以便在量子设备上初始化更大的、经典的难以模拟 MaxCut QAOA 问题实例。

The rest of the paper is organized as follows: Section 2 describes the preliminaries about the classical LSTM neural network, metalearning, and the quantum approximate optimization algorithm for the MaxCut QAOA problem; Section 3 presents the design focus of MetaQAOA, including the description of the MaxCut problem, the process of algorithm, and the method of optimizing parameter; in Section 4, experiments are performed in the quantum cloud computing platform to verify the feasibility of the proposed MetaQAOA; Section 5 summarizes what we ﬁnd in this work and the prospects for future research studies.

论文的其余部分组织如下：第 2 节介绍了经典 LSTM 神经网络、元学习和 MaxCut QAOA 问题的量子近似优化算法的预备知识；第3节介绍了MetaQAOA的设计重点，包括MaxCut问题的描述、算法流程和参数优化方法；第 4 节中在量子云计算平台上进行了实验，验证了所提出的 MetaQAOA 的可行性；第 5 节总结了本研究的发现和对未来研究的展望。。

2. Preliminaries

2.1 Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM)

In general, LSTM refers to long and short-term memory artiﬁcial neural network, which is a time loop neural network, and is used to solve the problem of long-term dependence. .ere are special units called memory blocks (shown in Figure 1) in the hidden layer of LSTM. In addition to special multiplicative units called gates to control the ﬂow of information, the memory blocks contain memory cells with self-connections storing the temporal state of the network. Each memory block contained an input gate, an output gate, and a forget gate [17]. The input gate controls the ﬂow of input activations into the memory cell. The output gate controls the output ﬂow of cell activations into the other part of the network. Because the forget gate can scale the cell before adding it as input to the cell through the self-connection of the cell, the cell’s memory is forgotten or reset adaptively. In addition, the current LSTM architecture (as shown in Figure 2) contains peephole connections from its internal cells to the gates in the same cell to understand the precise timing of the outputs [18].

2.2 Metalearning

Metalearning is to study how to design a machine learning model with less training set, so that it can learn well and quickly [19]. One specific example is a neural network model which learns how to optimize other neural network models with parameterized function. Metalearners can not only train machine learning models but also train and optimize general functions. In specific regions where models are used to optimize other models, early studies explored guided strategy search, which has been replaced by LSTMs [20]. LSTM is a kind of recurrent neural network, which is used to reduce the vanishing or exploding gradients in other recurrent neural network structures[21, 22]. An LSTM is composed of a cell state, a hidden state, and a gate, all of which are called LSTM cell. In each time step, the cell state is changed based on the hidden state, gate, and data information entered into the LSTM cell. The change of the hidden state depends on the gate and the input, and then, the cell state and hidden state are passed to the LSTM cell in the next time step. A complete processing method for LSTM is given in [23]. An LSTM is instrumental in learning long-term dependencies, such as those in optimization.

元学习是研究如何设计一个训练集较少的机器学习模型，使其能够很好地快速学习[19]。一个具体的例子是神经网络模型，它学习如何使用参数化函数优化其它神经网络模型。元学习器不仅可以训练机器学习模型，还可以训练和优化通用函数。在使用模型优化其它模型的特定区域，早期研究探索了引导策略搜索，已被 LSTM [20] 取代。LSTM 是一种循环神经网络，用于减少其它循环神经网络结构中的消失梯度或爆炸梯度[21, 22]。LSTM 由单元状态、隐藏状态和门组成，所有这些都称为 LSTM 单元。在每个时间步中，根据输入 LSTM 单元的隐藏状态、门和数据信息改变单元状态。隐藏状态的变化取决于门和输入，然后在下一个时间步将单元状态和隐藏状态传递给 LSTM 单元。LSTM的完整处理方法在[23]中给出。LSTM 有助于学习长期依赖关系，例如优化中的依赖关系。

Metalearners have been used in the rapid general optimization of the model, and there are few training examples[24]: given the random initial parameters, we seek to quickly converge to the “optimal” general parameters deﬁned by some measures. This same problem characteristic also appears in QAOA, and some common distribution across problems [25] may be followed by good parameters. A metalearner could be used to ﬁnd general good parameters and ﬁne-tuning left to some other optimizer [26].

元学习器已用于模型的快速通用优化，并且训练示例很少[24]：给定随机初始参数，我们寻求快速收敛到由一些度量定义的“最佳”通用参数。同样的问题特征也出现在 QAOA 中，并且一些问题之间的常见分布 [25] 可能会遵循良好的参数。一个元学习器可以用来寻找通用的好的参数并将其微调留给其它优化器[26]。

3. The Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm with Metalearning for the MaxCut Problem

The main purpose of the quantum optimization algorithm with metalearning for the MaxCut problem proposed in this paper is to train a classical long short-term memory(LSTM) network as an outer-loop optimizer to directly optimize the parameters in the QNN. In this section, we mainly introduce the process and training strategy of the MetaQAOA.

本文提出的求解 MaxCut 问题的元学习量子优化算法的主要目的是训练一个经典的长短期记忆（LSTM）网络作为外环优化器，直接优化 QNN 中的参数。本节主要介绍 MetaQAOA 的流程和训练策略。

3.1 MaxCutProblem

The MaxCut problem is to ﬁnd a split (S,V−SS, V − SS,V−S﻿) of the vertex set VVV﻿ so that the maximum number of edge weights is divided, i.e., one vertex of the edge is in SSS﻿ and the other vertex is in V−SV − SV−S﻿. This is a typical combination problem. Given a graph G(n,ε)G (n, ε)G(n,ε)﻿ with nodes nnn﻿ and edges {j,kj, kj,k﻿} ∈ε∈ ε∈ε﻿, ﬁnd a subset S∈nS ∈ nS∈n﻿ to maximize the number of edges between SSS﻿ and SSS﻿\nnn﻿. This problem can be attributed to ﬁnding the ground state of an Ising model.

MaxCut问题是求顶点集VVV﻿的一个分裂(S,V−SS,V-SS,V−S﻿)，使得边权重的最大个数被划分，即边的一个顶点在SSS﻿中，另一个顶点在V−SV-SV−S﻿中。这是一个典型的组合问题。给定一个带有节点 nnn﻿ 和边 {j,kj, kj,k﻿} ∈ εεε﻿ 的图 G(n,ε)G (n, ε)G(n,ε)﻿，找到一个子集 S∈nS ∈ nS∈n﻿ ,使 SSS﻿ 和 SSS﻿\nnn﻿ 之间的边数最大化。这个问题可以归为寻找伊辛模型的基态。

The simplest method is the enumeration method, which is the only method that can ﬁnd the global optimal solution (no approximation). However, this method is more complex, especially for the case of numerous vertices. Because when MaxCut only divides the vertices into two groups, there are a total of 2n2^n2n﻿ methods. Therefore, the MaxCut problem is an NP-hard problem.

最简单的方法是枚举法，它是唯一能找到全局最优解（无近似）的方法。但是这种方法比较复杂，特别是对于顶点较多的情况。因为当MaxCut只将顶点分成两组时，一共有2n2^n2n﻿种方法。因此，MaxCut 问题是一个 NP-hard 问题。

3.2. The Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm with Metalearning for the MaxCut Problem

The quantum approximate optimization algorithm with metalearning for the MaxCut problem (MetaQAOA) we proposed will be to train an optimizer network, i.e., metalearner, with metalearning to learn parameter update heuristics for QNN constructed in the form of the parameterized quantum circuit. The MetaQAOA is a local optimizer, which has a notion of location in the solution space. It can search for candidate solutions from this location. Besides, MetaQAOA is usually fast and is susceptible to ﬁnding local minima. Training an outer-loop optimizer on many instances of MaxCut problem to enhance the eﬃciency of solving unseen instances is the idea of metalearning for optimization. This may be to ensure that the learning is well generalized and that the model can better ﬁt the given data with fewer iterations. The algorithm we proposed mainly improves the update method of parameters in parameterized quantum circuits, and the steps of the proposed algorithm are shown in Algorithm 2.

我们提出的求解 MaxCut 问题（MetaQAOA）的基于元学习的量子近似优化算法将训练一个优化器网络，即元学习器，用元学习来学习以参数化量子电路形式构建的 QNN 的参数更新启发式。MetaQAOA 是一个局部优化器，它在解空间中具有位置概念。它可以从这个位置搜索候选解。此外，MetaQAOA 通常速度很快，并且容易找到局部最小值。在 MaxCut 问题的许多实例上训练外环优化器以提高解决未见实例的效率是优化元学习的想法。这可能是为了确保学习得到很好的泛化，并且模型可以用更少的迭代数来拟合给定的数据。我们提出的算法主要改进了参数化量子电路中参数的更新方法，所提出算法的步骤如算法2所示。

3.3. Training Strategy

In this section, we will provide more detailed information on how the MetaQAOA trains an LSTM to optimize the parameters of QNN. The goal of quantum-classical metalearning is to train LSTM to learn an eﬀective parameter update heuristics for a set of cost functions of interest, i.e., to ﬁnd an optimizer that eﬃciently optimizes the special distribution of optimization area on average. The better optimizer is an optimizer that ﬁnds the best approximate local minimum of the cost function in as few function queries as possible. The whole optimal qualiﬁcation is decided by the current problem category and the application area of interest.

在本节中，我们将提供有关 MetaQAOA 如何训练 LSTM 以优化 QNN 参数的更详细的内容。量子经典元学习的目标是训练 LSTM 学习一组感兴趣的成本函数的有效参数更新启发式，即找到一个优化器，平均有效地优化区域的特殊分布。较好的优化器是在尽可能少的函数查询中找到成本函数的最佳近似局部最小值的优化器。整个最佳资格由当前问题类别和感兴趣的应用领域决定。

In order to learn this general optimization strategy, the LSTM is trained by using a random instance, which has a fairly general distribution of functions, that is, functions are sampled from Gaussian processes. Since we focus on the known QNN optimization landscapes which are diﬀerent from the classical Gaussian process optimization landscapes, we aim to train neural optimizers for MaxCut QAOA problems and QNN ansatze. In order to explore the eﬀectiveness of our method, we will train the LSTM for random QNN instances in the target problem category, i.e., MaxCutQAOA, and test the trained network on (larger) previously unseen instances in the target problem category. The number of variational parameters in these ansatzes does not depend on the size of the system, but only on the number of alternating operators. This means we can use this training strategy to train our LSTM on smaller QNN instance sizes for MaxCut QAOA problems and then test for generalization on larger QNN instance sizes.

为了学习这种通用的优化策略，LSTM 使用随机实例进行训练，该随机实例具有相当普遍的函数分布，即函数是从高斯过程中采样的。由于我们关注的是已知的QNN优化问题，而不同于经典的高斯过程优化问题，我们的目标是训练用于 MaxCut QAOA 问题和 QNN 分析的神经优化器。为了探索该方法的有效性，我们将对目标问题类别中的随机 QNN 实例（即 MaxCut QAOA）训练 LSTM，并在目标问题类别中以前未见的（较大的）实例上测试训练过的网络。这些分析中变分参数的数量不取决于系统的大小，而仅取决于交替算子的数量。这意味着我们可以使用这种训练策略在较小的 QNN 实例大小上训练我们的 LSTM来解决 MaxCut QAOA 问题，然后在较大的 QNN 实例大小上测试泛化能力。

In order to evaluate the training performance ofthe algorithm proposed in this paper, some local optimizers, i.e., Nelder–Mead [11], L-BFGS-B [13], and our MetaQAOA, are compared. Nelder–Mead and L-BFGS-B are gradient-free and gradient-based approaches, respectively, which are standard local optimizers. L-BFGS-B is a local optimizer and has access to the gradients. Out of all optimizers chosen, it is the closest to the MetaQAOA in terms of information available to the optimizer and computational burden (i.e., the cost of computing the gradients). Nelder–Mead was chosen as it provides a widely recognized benchmark. Our MetaQAOA requires fewer iterations to reach the threshold of the loss function.

5. Conclusions

According to the shortcomings of weak generalization of the current methods of optimizing MaxCut QAOA circuits, we propose a quantum approximate optimization algorithm with metalearning for MaxCut problem (MetaQAOA) via TFQ platform. Our algorithm trains a classical LSTM network as an outer-loop optimizer to directly optimize the parameters in the QNN constructed by the parameterized quantum circuit. The simulation experiment results via TFQ show that MetaQAOA requires fewer iterations to reach the threshold of the loss function, and the threshold ofloss value after training is smaller than the other local optimization methods, i.e., Nelder–Mead and L-BFGS-B. In addition, our method can learn parameter update heuristics that generalize to larger system sizes and still outperform other initialization strategies of this scale. But the metalearning optimizer neural networks in our MetaQAOA cannot scale to arbitrary problems and number of parameters, and research that builds upon it might be addressed in future studies.

针对现有MaxCut QAOA电路优化方法泛化能力弱的缺点，在TFQ平台上提出了一种基于元学习的MaxCut问题量子近似优化算法（MetaQAOA）。我们的算法训练一个经典的LSTM网络作为外环优化器，以直接优化由参数化量子电路构造的 QNN 中的参数。通过 TFQ 进行的仿真实验结果表明，MetaQAOA 达到损失函数阈值所需的迭代次数较少，训练后的损失值阈值小于其它局部优化方法，即 Nelder-Mead 和 L-BFGS-B。此外，我们的方法可以学习到更大系统规模的参数更新启发式方法，并且仍然优于这种规模的其它初始化策略。但是我们的 MetaQAOA 中的元学习优化器神经网络无法扩展到任意问题和参数数量，在此基础上的研究可能会在未来的研究中得到解决。
